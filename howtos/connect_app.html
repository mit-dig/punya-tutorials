Connect with your device over wifi

These instructions will show you how to connect using the MIT Punya Companion app, so you can see your app on your mobile device.

Download and install the MIT Punya Companion App on your phone

Download by scanning the QR code below or by opening this link on your device.

Follow the installation instructions on your phone

After downloading, step though the the instructions to install the Companion app on your device. You only need to install the MIT Punya Companion once. Then, you can leave it on your phone or tablet for whenever you use MIT Punya.

Open your app's QR code

Click Connect, then Companion.

Connect your app with your phone

A QR code similar to this should pop up on your screen.

You can scan the QR code or enter the code in the text box.
